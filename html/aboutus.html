About

This Personal Task Manager Is A Simple Tool Designed To Help You Organize Your Daily Tasks. Create Tasks, Set Deadlines, Mark Tasks As Completed, And Stay Productive With Ease.

How To Use

  1. **Add a Task**
    • Click the **"Add Task"** button on the Dashboard.
    • Enter a title, description, and optional due date.
    • Click **Save**.
  2. **Mark as Completed**
    • From the **Dashboard**, click the checkbox next to any task.
  3. **Edit a Task**
    • Select a task to open **Task Details**.
    • Click **Edit**, update the information, and save.
  4. **Delete a Task**
    • In **Task Details**, press the **Delete** button.
    • Confirm deletion.
